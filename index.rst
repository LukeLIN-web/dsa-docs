Welcome to DSAGEN's documentation!
==================================

DSAGEN is a framework for designing decoupled-spatial architectures, a
class of programmable accelerators.  DSAGEN makes use of a variety of tools
for spatial-scheduling, compilation, simulation, ISA-generation, and hardware
generation.
